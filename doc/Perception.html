    Perception: Human perceptible printouts

    Welcome to Perception. Itâ€™s a intuitive toolkit for simple everyday debugging. Perception offers a powerfull replacement for print, puts and pp. You get auto-intended and structured output on your console without any configuration. Perception also offers temporary printouts (overwritten by the next print), printing two informations in one line without destroying the structure and optionally slows down printing to readable speed, depending on the complexity of the output.
